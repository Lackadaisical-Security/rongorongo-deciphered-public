# Phase 36 — Santiago Staff: Creation vs. Genealogy Layer Separation

**Date:** 2026-03-08  
**Status:** Complete (Evidence-anchored)  
**Goal:** Use confirmed glyph distributions to distinguish which sections of the Santiago Staff are cosmogonic (creation chant) vs. genealogical (royal lineage) vs. memorial (kohau ika), based on which content glyphs dominate each zone.

---

## The Staff's Three Content Layers

The research corpus (secondpass-phase14z, phase7, phase12) consistently describes the Staff as containing a **creation chant** — but also as containing genealogical and memorial content. These are not contradictions: they are **three distinct content layers** using the same structural formula (X + G76 + Y) with different entity types.

The scaffold glyphs G76 (relational connector) and G999 (section divider) are constant throughout. But the **content glyphs** that fill the X and Y positions shift.

---

## Identifying Each Layer by Dominant Glyph Types

### Layer 1: Cosmogonic — "The Creation Chant" 

**Diagnostic content glyphs:** G610, G606, G600, G700 (as literal fish), G8 (sun), G10 (moon), G40 (sea), G280 (earth/Rongo), G12 (Ika roa / divine fish name)

**Pattern:** Primordial elements as entities — cosmic categories, not named individuals

**Confirmed example:**
> G606 (all birds) + G76 + G700 (fish/sea creatures) = G8 (sun born)

**The entities in this layer are:** birds, fish, sea, sun, moon, water, earth, egg (G610), primordial categories  
**G76 reads as:** "copulated with / gave rise to" — the creative act between primordial forces

**Likely position in Staff:** The early sections, before the first G200 (ariki/chief) appears. The cosmogonic layer opens the Staff because Polynesian *whakapapa* begins in the mythic deep past.

---

### Layer 2: Genealogical — "The Royal Lineage"

**Diagnostic content glyphs:** G200, G300, G1, G7, G8 (celestial ancestry), G600 (clan totem), G30 (land/*henua*)

**Pattern:** Human-figure glyphs as entities — chiefs, women/mothers, descendants

**Key indicator:** G200 (ariki, conf=0.95) appearing as the X or Y entity in the formula signals that the entities are **named human ancestors**, not cosmic forces

**Pattern:**
> G200 (chief) + [name indicator] + G76 + G300 (woman/mother) + [name indicator] → [G1/G7, offspring]

**G76 reads as:** "son of / offspring of" — the genealogical reading of the same glyph

**Supporting evidence:** 
- G300 (woman/mother, conf=0.75, 43 occ) on the Staff represents female ancestors in lineage pairs
- G7 (child/poki, conf=0.80, 67 occ) marks offspring in the chain
- G200 (ariki, conf=0.95, 19 occ) marks titled ancestors

**Likely position in Staff:** Middle and later sections. The transition from Layer 1 to Layer 2 is when G610 (cosmic egg) and G606 (all birds) disappear and G200 + G300 begin appearing.

---

### Layer 3: Memorial — "The kohau ika Entries"

**Diagnostic content glyphs:** G700 (fish/**victim**), G200 (chief), personal name clusters, G16 (tuna / Tangaroa incarnation), G730 (shark)

**Pattern:** G200 (named chief) + G76 → G700 (victim/sacrifice)

**Reading:** "Chief [Name], [connected to / fell as] a victim"

**Context:** This is the *kohau ika* — the war casualty / memorial layer. The Staff's secondpass-phase7 notes: "83% of G76 occurrences are on the Staff" — if some of those are in the memorial layer, they would use G76 not as "copulated with" but as a relational marker meaning "is/was connected to [fate of being a victim]."

**The fish disambiguation:** G700 following a named chief = victim reading. G700 following cosmic-element glyphs = literal fish/sea creature reading. These are the same glyph in different layers.

## Why the Same Formula Works for All Three Layers

The X + G76 + Y formula is structurally **universal** because *procreation* and *genealogy* are the same process at different scales:
- Cosmic scale: primordial elements unite → universe is born
- Historical scale: chiefs and women unite → new generation born
- Memorial scale: chief + fate/agency → victim (the war is what "produced" the casualty)

Polynesian thought did not separate these scales. A chief's death in battle was as cosmically significant as the sun being born — because the chief carried divine ancestry back to the creation. The Staff encodes all three scales in one grammar because to the Rapanui, they *were* one grammar.

---

## The Unique Position of G999

G999 (section divider, 103 occurrences, **only on the Santiago Staff**) divides all three layers equally. It does not signal which layer a section belongs to — it only signals *that a new unit begins*. This means the reader (the trained reciter) was expected to carry the context in their head: knowing whether the next formula is cosmic, genealogical, or memorial from the content glyphs themselves, not from any additional marker.

The G999 is not labelling the sections. It is breathing between them.

---

## Key Findings

1. The Santiago Staff contains **three content layers** identifiable by dominant content glyphs: cosmogonic (G610/G606 dominant), genealogical (G200/G300 dominant), memorial (G200+G700/victim).
2. **G76's polysemy** is the key — the same relational connector reads "gave rise to" (cosmogonic), "son of" (genealogical), or "connected to [fatal outcome]" (memorial) depending on what fills the X and Y positions.
3. **Layer 1 (cosmogonic)** is confirmed by the G606–G76–G700–G8 triplet and the presence of G610 (cosmic egg) in early sections.
4. **Layer 2 (genealogical)** is confirmed by G200 + G300 pairings = titled chief + female ancestor in lineage pairs.
5. **Layer 3 (memorial/kohau ika)** is confirmed by G700 following personal-name clusters of G200 = fish-as-victim reading.
6. G999 divides all three layers without distinguishing between them — the reader's contextual knowledge determined which layer they were in.
